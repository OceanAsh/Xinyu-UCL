Work Experiences

I’m a person who loves going on new adventures and learning new things. After graduation, I took a gap year and had two internships including working at a company called Nanjing Electronic Devices Institute, where I worked on different projects and problem-solving skills. The first project was its new digital library project. I sorted and extracted data using SQL and then work with the development team to come up with system design. Another project was its online employee platform, where I used java and HTML to program and test web pages. The other internship is working as a data analyst. I conducted system tests and worked with other teams to analyse customers’ needs and upgrade the system. I was able to combine the customers’ needs with the system function and designed a new feature for the system. I’ve also worked as an analytic intern at Siemens for a summer internship and I analysed the customers’ data trend and used Tableau to demonstrate how sales volume changed over each quarter. Additionally, through practical coursework at Penn State IST department, I examined organisational strategy and enterprise architecture along with different case studies to study different ERP systems. I then became the learning assistant for one of my IST courses for a year. The class had about 100 students and was designed for junior students who are interested in studying how information technology should support an organisation. During that time, I was able to take initiative to help my professor make PowerPoint slides for the class. I’ve also gained strong verbal communication skills by taking students’ opinions about the midterm exam and organised a review session afterwards. Moreover, when my professor was out of town for a conference, I took the responsibility and lectured the students.
